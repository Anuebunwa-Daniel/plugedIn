Solving the Biggest Problem in the Service-Hiring Industry: Zero Consumer Protection.

Existing platforms expose clients to unverified workers, high risk of scam, poor work quality, and no financial protection. Pluggedin fixes all that.

1
Escrow-Based Payment System

When a user hires a professional, Pluggedin securely holds the payment. Funds are only released after job completion + 24‑hour satisfaction confirmation.

2
Before & After Photo Verification

Professionals must upload before & after photos. This protects clients and resolves disputes fairly and transparently.

3
Automatic Payout After 24 Hours

If the client raises no complaint, payment is released to the professional automatically — reducing scam risk to 0%.
